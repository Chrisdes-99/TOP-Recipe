Burger

Description

A burger is held together by two buns on its north and south pole. Between the buns typically include meat, lettuce, tomatoe, pickles, and ketchup

Ingredients

  • Two Buns
  • Meat
  • Lettuce
  • Mayo
  • Ketchup
  • Tomatoe
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook Meat
  2. Place cooked meat on bottom half bun
  3. Place cheese on meat
  4. Then on top of that, place lettuce and tomatoe in disired order.
  5. Place ketchup, mayo anywhere on burgers
